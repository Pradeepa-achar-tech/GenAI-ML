              theory: '**A CNN block** has three components:\n• **`Conv2D(in_ch, out_ch, kernel=3, padding=1)`** — slides `out_ch` learnable 3×3 filters across the input. Massively stabilises training; allows higher learning rates.\n• **ReLU / GELU / SiLU** — non-linearity.
